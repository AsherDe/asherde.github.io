关于高数教材的思考

Posted on 2023-11-14 Edited on 2023-11-20

The textbooks for high math take simple definitions and proofs of millennial problems that have a common denominator, and they all look so lofty that they are simply unreadable. I thought the textbook was written for students to read,wihile students to read in pain, how rigorous are your definitions? I don’t know, but as a student, I assert that he is failing the textbook.
Even if you abandon part of the rigor, you should come to do simplification, so that students can feel the beauty of mathematics, rather than a bunch of miscellaneous details to block the footsteps of students to move forward to explore.
Mathematicians also start out with rough definitions, which are subsequently refined, so what’s the reason to let the student, who is exposed to him for the first time, directly understand the final definition that is ultimately the most perfect?
I’m not qualified to judge the intellectual achievements of experts; after all, I’m still a student. But he confuses me, and after a moment of enlightenment I realize that most of it is nonsense.
Zhang Xiaolong said: every day someone wants to teach him how to make WeChat better, the user is neither a designer, do not understand WeChat’s every detail of the design of how ingenious, nor a programmer, do not know how complex the implementation of each function. But the user gives you the best feedback, they use your products, like trying shoes with their feet, good wear bad wear users know best. Only your shoes do not meet the user’s feet, you go to change, there is no user’s feet do not fit your shoes, the user to cut off the heel of the reason. But Tencent is such a big family, like South Korea’s plutocrats big but not fall, let the user walk in crappy shoes is the biggest insult to socialism.
By the same token, the experts should look at what they are doing and what kind of youth they are educating. I don’t need to talk about the numbers of Chinese youth depression rate, I have seen with my own eyes that high schools educate machine-like college students who don’t even have any idea about their future, anesthetizing themselves in short videos and games to cope with every exam. I have a question, is the foot not fit your shoes?
